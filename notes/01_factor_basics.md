# Factor Basics

## What is a factor?

A factor is a signal or characteristic assigned to each stock that may help predict future returns.

In practice, factor research often means:

1. define a measurable signal
2. compute it for many stocks
3. compare it with future returns
4. check whether the signal has predictive power

## Examples of common factors

- momentum
- value
- size
- quality
- low volatility

## My understanding

A factor is essentially a way to rank stocks.  
If stocks with higher factor values tend to have better future returns, then the factor may be useful.
